Emergency Procedures - Checklist

ASSIST
  • Acknowledge the emergency call
  • Separate the emergency traffic from other traffic
  • Silence on the frequency
  • Inform adjecent units and sectors
  • Support the pilot by providing room for manoeuvre and provide relavant information
  • Time - give the pilot time to make good decisions
